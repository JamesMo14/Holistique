FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any experience to join this course?
No prior experience necessary. The course accommodates complete beginners and wellness enthusiasts or practitioners seeking deeper knowledge, with step-by-step guidance throughout.
Will I be able to needle other people after taking this course?
This course targets personal use exclusively. Informal use with family or close friends (done safely with consent) is acceptable, but you cannot offer paid acupuncture services or claim practitioner status. UK professional practice requires certified training and insurance.
Is this course accredited or certified?
This course does not offer a formal qualification. It is designed for personal development, self-care, and wellness education. A completion certificate may be provided but it is not a professional practice licence.
Can I do the course online or in person?
Currently the course is in-person only. Sessions feature interactive, practical instruction with demonstrations and Q&A opportunities in Kensington, London.
What’s included in the course?
A digital PDF guide with diagrams and safety information, a recommended tools and suppliers list, sterile needles provided during training sessions, and ongoing support for questions and guidance between sessions.
Will I learn how to choose the right acupuncture points for different conditions?
Yes. You’ll learn how to locate and use points for stress, anxiety, relaxation, headaches, muscular pain, sleep, energy balance, digestion, immunity, and more—always emphasising safe personal use.
Do I need my own acupuncture needles?
No needles are required beforehand. Sterile, single-use needles are provided during training. Additional packs are available for purchase. The digital guide includes trusted suppliers, needle type guidance, and safe storage and disposal tips.
Is self-acupuncture safe?
Generally safe when performed correctly with proper guidance. The course teaches proper hygiene, safety techniques, and helps you recognise when acupuncture supports natural body healing.
